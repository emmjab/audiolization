Audiolyzer

Listen to your data.

Audiolyzer is an alternative to visualizing data by turning the visual parameters into sound.

Audience

Our audience is comprised of visually impaired users. In the U.S. alone, there are 6,670,300 blind adults.

In the future, we hope that radio stations, podcasts and other audio outlets will be able to use this software.
